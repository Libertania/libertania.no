CREATING A NEW CITY

An ambitious project to make a new city from scratch, located in Norway.

About Libertania

Some words about the project

It's an ambitious project to make a new city from scratch. But that is our goal. We are determined to make this happen whatever it costs, and whatever obstacles that may come in our way.

  • Freedom - Improve your personal freedom
  • Voluntarily - All city interactions should be of mutual consent
  • Life - Enjoy the life you want

Location

While many might not realize this, Norway is a good place to start building a new city for many reasons.

One of those reasons, is the population density of the country. Norway is ranked together with countries such as Australia, known for it's widespread dessolate deserts and Greenland for it's huge massive ice and snow covered areas.

Libertania will be built in Norway, which is covered with green forests and abundance of fresh clean water.


Who We Are

We are a small group of individuals who are seeking a change in the way society works. We have had enough of governments trying to control, regulate, and force people to comply with laws and rules that has no place in a civilized society. We want to start making a better world for everybody. A place where people can be free to prosper and thrive. A place where people can reach their full potential, without a government telling them what they can or can not do.
